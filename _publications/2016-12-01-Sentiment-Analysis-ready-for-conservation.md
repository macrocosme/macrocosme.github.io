<b>Write Back.</b> 

We welcome the paper by Ladle et al.(2016), as it brings attention to the important topic of 
understanding social responses and cultural trends through the analysis of digital texts. The 
availability of these data, along with the number of tools created to analyze them, has 
increased immensely with the arrival of, and growing access to, the internet. We agree with 
Ladle et al.(2016) that conservation scientists have only just begun to tap into this wealth of 
data. One topic they mention briefly is the analysis of sentiment, which is described as 
“particularly difficult to automatically assess, owing to the semantic complexity of language 
and the frequent use of abbreviations, misnomers, misspellings, slang, and sarcasm”(Ladle
