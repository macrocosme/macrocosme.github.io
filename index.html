Foreword

Thank you for landing on my personal research site. If you do not readily know me, here is a brief introduction to myself and my academic background.

I am a researcher operating in the fields of astronomy and astrophysics. I investigate questions related to high-energy (extra)(g/G)alactic astrophysics, compact objects (neutron stars, black holes), and stellar & galaxy formation and evolution. I am a specialist in scientific software design and implementation for data maneuver, visualization, and (statistical) analysis. I completed my doctoral research in Melbourne, Australia at the Centre for Astrophysics & Supercomputing, Swinburne University of Technology. I am now based in the Netherlands since 2018, with research at ASTRON, the Netherlands Institute for Radio Astronomy, and the Anton Pannekoek Institute for Astronomy at the University of Amsterdam. I originate from Québec, Canada. My mother tongue is French, I commonly speak English, en ik probeer Nederlands te leren.

A list of my scientific publications, presentations, training material and software can be accessed under the work section.

In the hope that this page can be useful to some of you.
- D.V.
