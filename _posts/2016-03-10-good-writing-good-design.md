Writing is part of today's and yesterday's researchers lives. It's how we communicate our ideas (mostly).
Writing well requires clarity of ideas. I think that the clarity of an idea requires it to be well designed.
It usually takes a few revolution or drafts before being achieved. Writing code is similar.
It's easy to write a piece of code that runs. It's hard to make code readable, concise, and fast.

Writing a paper about code, it seems to me, requires good designs at both ends.
