While reading on the topic of [shader](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shader) in the past weeks, I discovered a fun place on the web :
[Shadertoy](https://www.shadertoy.com). Put simply, a shader is a program that tells how to
display something on your screen.

Shadertoy lets you develop shader online, and render in real-time using webgl.
