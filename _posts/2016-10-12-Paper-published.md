In a [previous post](https://macrocosme.github.io/Paper-submitted/), I was discussing that I submitted a paper to PeerJ 
Computer Science. Well, this very paper is now published! You can find all the details at 
[https://peerj.com/articles/cs-88/?td=bl](https://peerj.com/articles/cs-88/?td=bl). 

The paper changed slightly since its first submission, but the core of the work stayed the same. I added some extra
 details and a timing experiment section based on reviewers feedback. 
 
 PeerJ Computer Science publications are very dynamic. You can ask questions about paragraphs, about the paper; 
 download specific figures, cite tables or figures with unique DOI, etc. And it is open access. 
 I am very happy to see it published there.
